Warriors Sol is a Strategy TD (Tower Defense) game with a unique style in its genre. Set in a magical world you will have to upgrade your defenses and mighty soldiers to survive and siege enemy territories to conquer them.

The rival alliance of the evil kingdoms has pushed what remains of the human civilization to a corner of the world, isolated and forgotten, plunged into misery and trying to survive harshly.
